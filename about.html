His Story

Fatwell Mugadia is a Kenyan gospel minister and songwriter based in Nairobi. A husband, a father, and a man of faith — he writes and sings in Swahili, choosing the language he prays in to craft songs that feel honest rather than performed.

His debut EP, Nimemuona Yahweh — "I have seen Yahweh" — released in July 2021, carries five songs recorded as both confession and testimony. The tracklist moves deliberately: from an opening declaration of having seen God, through forgiveness, a question of what to give, weeping, and finally a letter.

More than a songwriter, Fatwell is a minister — someone who believes music is one of the clearest ways a person can speak truth to another person's spirit.
